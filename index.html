Project ideas

Overview

My project will relate to both fields that I interest in. It is called S.A.M which means Smart Android Manager. SAM is a virtual assistant and integrated into an Android phone just like SIRI of IOS. Plus, it can control the IoT network in our home. SAM will learn from user habits to make their daily life easier in many ways. To give customers the best experience, the AI will design itself to be suitable. For example, it can change names, genders, languages and accents. SAM will make the user feel like they have a real partner.

Motivation

There are two reasons that inspire me to make this project. The first one is updating the Google Assistance and second is helping users to manage their daily life. The Google Assistance or even other AIs can only follow user requests then execute that order. That makes it become very passive. What I want is the assistance can learn and adapt to their customer’s habits. Moreover, it should be active when the user shouts its name out, not by button. For example, I normally travel to school in one way. On a random day, there is an accident or that stress is constructed, it will update for me to take other routes. For the second reason, people are becoming busier day by day. It is very lucky for someone can manage or remain them to do. Due to many appointments one may have, a person may make two appointments which occur simultaneously or forget it. I want to create an AI that can access insight other applications and convert every date that the user has made to its cloud. In addition, an idea is that make an active AI for customers.

Description

As I described in the Overview, SAM will be integrated on our Android devices and also control the house’s IoT network. It will store user information and other materials, such as timetables and appointments in Google Cloud which use Google account to identify. Some customers need more security like, business. There will be a payable pack for private Cloud where only Google and that agency can access. For the first contact with a new user, it will ask for types of accents and genders that the owner prefers. Then, when SAM has been generated, it will execute some deeper functions to help the user as a real manager:

Learn to help: In the modern world, 24 hours are usually too short to finish everything in perfect condition. People try to make it fast then heading home or hanging out. Frequently, some bad habits will occur. For instance, programmers and designers are normally working at night so they do not have much time to sleep. They usually have a problem waking early and getting late for events. SAM will deal with this problem in steps by steps. Firstly, it will have a right to access to all communicated applications such as Gmail, phone and message. Then, when you make a call or send a mail that relates to make a date in the morning. Finally, it will considerate that the user will have enough sleep for the night before by making a remind on voice at that night. If the owner is need to work late, SAM will immediately remind after finish a mail or a call.

Learn to serve: - Ready to serve: SAM holds user’s schedules of a day. It will use the IoT network to make all machines work automatically. For example, the owner usually heads home at 6 pm, the AC will be activated at 5:45 pm. Moreover, based on the environment, the temperature will be calculatedly altered. For instance, in Summer, AC will be used and heater will be operated in Winter. The light will be adjusted to be appropriate with the activities of the owner. To illustrate it, at the time from 9 pm to 1 am, the user is working so the light will always at the brightest level.

With the AI system, SAM can do even more. However, these are the two main functions make SAM different, turn it to a project that can bring the best experience to clients.

Tools and Technologies

For the brain of SAM, the Google Cloud AI Platform will be very suitable for developing. I am aiming to update the AI of Google so using their platform might notice my project. Firstly, the required software. Based on DevTeam.Space, when using Google Cloud AI Platform, we can easily approach machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, speech and vision. All of them are the basics of an AI must have. For the IoT network, it needs to be secured at high level. Using some identity services engines, firewall and software defined like Cisco to take control the network. Secondly, the required hardware. The AI accelerator is a microprocessor and it is also a brain of the IoT network. The IoT network need route of Internet throughout electronic devices like AC and light bulbs. Therefore, a wireless modem is necessary.

Skills Required

With this project, I need to have experience in Big Data, Machine learning, deep learning and algorithms/analysts. Coding skill in Python needed to be proficient or other areas, such as TensorFlow, Keras, OpenCV, Machine Learning Fundamentals. Intermediate knowledge about computer system like circuit installation and assembling.

However, if this project came true, I would like to sale it at the price that everyone can afford. By that, many people life would be better. They can arrange their time more logically. The user will no longer need to worry about forgetting things that they would normally do. In conclusion, with virtual manager named SAM, user’s issues will never be a burden anymore.
